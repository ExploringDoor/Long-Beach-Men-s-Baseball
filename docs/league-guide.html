Long Beach Men's 50+ Baseball — Your Website Guide
🌐 long-beach-men-s-baseball.vercel.app

Welcome to the official Long Beach Diamond Classics website! This is your home for everything league-related — scores, stats, schedules, standings, team info, and more. Bookmark it on your phone and check back every week. This guide walks you through every single section so you know exactly where to find what you're looking for.

Questions or requests? Contact the league commissioner or email adam.mainlinewebdesign@gmail.com — anything you need updated on the site will be handled ASAP.

📱
How to Open the Website
1️⃣

On your phone — open Safari or Chrome

Tap the browser app on your phone (the compass icon for Safari, or the colorful circle for Chrome).

2️⃣

Type in the address

Tap the address bar at the top and type: long-beach-men-s-baseball.vercel.app

Then press Go on your keyboard.

3️⃣

Save it as a bookmark!

On iPhone: tap the Share button (the box with an arrow pointing up) → then tap "Add to Home Screen". It'll appear on your home screen just like a regular app!

💡 Tip: The site works great on your phone — everything is designed for mobile. No need to zoom in or squint at tiny text.
🧭
The Navigation Bar — Your Map

At the top of every page you'll see the navigation menu. On a computer it looks like this:

On your phone, the same menu is at the top — just scroll it left and right to see all the options.

HOME — The Main Page
Start Here
📋

Recent Results

The home page shows the most recent game results right when you open the site. You'll see final scores, who won, and a Recap and Box Score button for each game.

📊

Standings Snapshot

On the right side (or below on phone) you'll see a quick standings summary showing where every team sits in the current season — wins, losses, and record.

📅

Top Ticker Bar

At the very top of the page there's a dark bar showing today's games with the game time — so you know at a glance what's happening that day.

📋
SCORES — Game Results
Most Used
🗓️

Pick Your Season

At the top you'll see tabs for different seasons (e.g., Spring/Summer 2026, Fall/Winter 2026). Tap the season you want to see.

📅

Pick a Date

Once you pick a season, you'll see date buttons at the top (Apr 11, Apr 18, May 2, etc.). Tap any date to see the games from that week.

🏆

Read the Score Card

Each game shows up as a card with both teams and the final score. The winning team's score is bold. A headline in red across the top tells you what happened.

FINAL      GENERALS CHAMPS!!
GENERALS10
DODGERS6
📰 RECAP
🏟️ BOX SCORE
📰

RECAP Button

Tapping RECAP gives you a short written summary of what happened in the game.

🏟️

BOX SCORE Button

Tapping BOX SCORE opens a full window with every player's batting stats (AB, R, H, RBI, BB, K) and the pitching stats. You can tap any player's name in the box score to pull up their career stats!

📅
SCHEDULE — Upcoming Games
🏟️

See All Your Games for the Season

The Schedule tab shows the full season game by game. Tap any date at the top to jump to that week. Each game shows the teams, time, and field location.

📍

Field Location

Each game lists the field — for example "Clark Field — Long Beach" or "St Pius X — Downey". Know where you're going before game day!

📲

Add Games to Your Calendar

On each team's page you'll find a button to Subscribe to your team's Google Calendar — your games will automatically appear in your phone's calendar!

⚠️ Managers: If a game time, date, or location ever changes and needs to be updated on the calendar, please reach out to the commissioner or email adam.mainlinewebdesign@gmail.com so it can be updated — that way it reflects correctly on everyone's phone.

💡 Note: Away team is listed first, home team second. For example: "Pirates @ Brooklyn" means Pirates are visiting Brooklyn's field.
🏆
STANDINGS — League Rankings
📊

Current Season Standings

See exactly where every team stands in the league — wins (W), losses (L), winning percentage (PCT), runs scored, runs against, and run differential. Updates automatically after each game is entered.

📜

Season History Tab

Click "Season History" at the top to see final standings from every past season going all the way back to 2019!

💡 What do the columns mean?
W = Wins · L = Losses · T = Ties · PCT = Win % · GP = Games Played · RS = Runs Scored · RA = Runs Against · DIFF = Run Differential
⚾
TEAMS — Team Directory
🔍

Find Your Team

The Teams page shows all 7 teams — Tribe, Dodgers, Pirates, Titans, Brooklyn, Generals, and Black Sox. You'll see each team's current record and stats at a glance.

👥

View Team Page

Click "View Team →" on any team card to go to that team's dedicated page. There you'll see: roster, full schedule, season record, and all game results for that team.

📲

Subscribe to Team Calendar

On every team card there's a "Subscribe" button. Tap it to add that team's games to your Google Calendar. Never miss a game!

Is there anything else you'd like added or updated on the site? Email adam.mainlinewebdesign@gmail.com and it'll get taken care of ASAP!

📈
STATS — Player Leaderboards
Fan Favorite
🥇

Batting Leaderboard

See every player ranked by batting average. Shows AB, Hits, AVG, OBP, SLG, R, RBI, 2B, 3B, HR, BB, K. Tap any column header to sort by that stat — e.g., tap "HR" to see who has the most home runs!

⚾

Pitching Leaderboard

Click the PITCHING tab to see pitcher stats — IP, H, R, ER, BB, SO, ERA, and win/loss record.

🔎

Search & Filter

Use the search box to find any specific player or team. Use the season dropdown to switch between seasons — stats go all the way back to 2021!

👤

Tap a Player's Name

Click on any player's name in the stats table to see their full career stats — all seasons combined, game log, and best performances.

📜
HISTORY — The Archive
🏅

Every Season Going Back to 2019

The History page is the full archive — 35+ seasons, 570+ games. On the left side you'll see a list of every season. Click any season to see that year's standings, rosters, and results.

🏆

Tournaments & Special Events

Click the "Tournaments & Special Events" tab to see NABA World Series results, Turkey Bowl tournaments, and other special events the guys have participated in.

📖
RULES — Field Guide
⚖️

Official League Rules

This page has all the official rules for the 2026 season — Playoff Eligibility, Mercy Rule & Time Limit, Courtesy Runners, Defensive Shifts, Rosters & Age requirements, and St. Pius X Ground Rules.

🔗

Jump Links

At the top of the Rules page are clickable buttons for each section — tap any one to jump straight to that rule. No scrolling required.

📌 Key rule reminder: Players must participate in a minimum of 4 games to be eligible for playoffs. This is tracked automatically through box scores!
