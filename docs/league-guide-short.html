Add Games to Your Calendar
  1. Go to the Schedule tab
  2. Tap any game to open its details
  3. Tap "Add to Calendar" — it saves directly to your phone or Google Calendar
  4. Want the whole season at once? Tap "Subscribe to Team Calendar" and every game updates automatically
📅 Need your team's calendar link? Contact your team manager or reach out to adam.mainlinewebdesign@gmail.com and we'll get it to you.
