How to Enter a Box Score After Your Game
Team Captains Only
⏰ Please submit box scores within 24 hours of your game. Stats update automatically once you hit Submit — the whole league can see them right away.
Step-by-Step
1

Log In

Go to the website → tap Admin → select your team from the dropdown. No password needed.

2

Pick Your Game

Your recent games appear at the top. Tap the correct one to open it.

3

Enter the Final Score

Type in the runs scored for each team.

4

Enter the Linescore

Runs per inning for both teams. Tap each inning box and type the number.

5

Set the Batting Order

Tap Full Stats — you'll be prompted to set the batting order. Tap players 1 through 9 in the order they batted. Tap a player again to remove them if you made a mistake.

6

Enter Batting Stats

Use the + and − buttons next to each player to enter: AB, H, R, RBI, BB, 2B, 3B, HR. Repeat for both teams.

7

Enter Pitching Stats

Tap the Pitching tab. Enter IP, H, R, ER, BB, K for each pitcher.

8

Add a Recap (Optional)

Write a quick sentence or two about the game — highlights, big plays, walk-offs. Totally optional but fun for the league.

9

Hit Submit!

Tap the green Submit button at the bottom. Done! Stats go live immediately.

Made a mistake? You can always come back and edit — just log back in, find the game, and make your changes.
