How to Submit a Box Score
Enter game stats within 24 hours of the final out
When to submit Box scores should be submitted within 24 hours of the game ending. Stats appear on the site immediately after submission.
1

Log in to the Captain Portal

Follow Section 1 above. From the Captain Portal menu, tap Submit Box Score (the gray card with 📋).

2

Select the game from the dropdown

A dropdown will show today's scheduled games for your team. Select the game you just played. The opponent, date, and field should pre-fill automatically.

If you don't see your game listed, it may not be scheduled yet — contact the commissioner.

3

Enter the final score

Type the runs scored by each team in the score fields. Double-check that you have the home and away teams correct before continuing.

4

Enter each batter's stats

For each player who batted, enter their line: AB (At-Bats), H (Hits), HR (Home Runs), RBI, BB (Walks), and K (Strikeouts). Only enter players who actually appeared.

5

Enter pitcher stats

Scroll down to the pitching section. For each pitcher used, enter: IP (Innings Pitched), H, ER (Earned Runs), BB, and K.

6

Tap Submit

When all stats look correct, scroll to the bottom and tap the Submit button. The box score will be saved and stats will appear on the website immediately.

You'll see a confirmation message when submission is successful.

Adding a Player to Your Lineup
Add a walk-up player before submitting a box score
Player must be on roster first Players only appear in the box score dropdowns if they are on your team's roster. If someone shows up game-day who isn't listed, add them to the roster first, then submit the box score.
1

Log in to the Captain Portal

Before submitting the box score, go to the Captain Portal (see Section 1).

2

Tap "Manage Roster"

From the Captain Portal menu, tap the green Manage Roster card (👥).

3

Tap "+ Add Player"

In the top-right corner of the roster screen, tap the + Add Player button. An input form will appear.

4

Enter their jersey number and name

Type their jersey # (optional) and their full name. Tap Save.

The # field is optional — you can leave it blank and fill it in later.

5

Go back to Submit Box Score

Tap ← Back to return to the Captain Portal menu, then tap Submit Box Score. The new player will now appear in the player dropdowns.

Eligibility Note Adding a player to your roster allows them to appear in box scores. Make sure any new players meet the league's eligibility requirements. Contact the commissioner if you have questions about a player's eligibility.

2

Add a new player

Tap + Add Player in the top right. A small form appears — enter the player's jersey number (optional) and name, then tap Save. The player is immediately added to your roster.

3

Edit an existing player

Tap the Edit button next to any player's name. Update their number or name in the form that appears, then tap Save.

4

Remove a player

Tap the red ✕ button next to a player's name. A confirmation prompt will appear — tap OK to confirm the removal.

Removing a player from the roster does not delete their historical stats from past box scores.

Changes save automatically Roster changes take effect immediately — there is no separate "Save Roster" step. The player will appear (or disappear) from box score entry dropdowns right away.

Adding the Schedule to Your Phone Calendar
iPhone · Android · Google Calendar
Your team has a dedicated Google Calendar Each team has its own calendar feed. When you add it, all of your team's games automatically appear in your calendar app — and update if the schedule changes.
Option A — iPhone / Apple Calendar (Recommended)
1

Open Safari on your iPhone

This must be done in Safari — it will not work from Chrome or other browsers on iPhone.

2

Go to lbdc.vercel.app and open the Schedule page

Tap the Schedule tab, then find your team's section. Look for the Add to Calendar button or a calendar icon next to your team name.

3

Tap the calendar link for your team

When you tap the calendar link for your team, Safari will ask: "Subscribe to Calendar?" — tap Subscribe. Your team's full schedule will be imported into the Apple Calendar app.

4

Done! Open your Calendar app to confirm

Open the built-in Calendar app on your iPhone. You should see a new calendar called something like "LBDC — [Your Team]" with all game dates, times, and fields pre-filled.

The calendar will stay in sync — if game times change or games are added, your phone calendar updates automatically.

Option B — Android / Google Calendar
1

Open Google Calendar on a computer or Android phone

Go to calendar.google.com or open the Google Calendar app.

2

Tap the calendar link for your team on lbdc.vercel.app

On the Schedule page, tap the Google Calendar link next to your team. This will open Google Calendar with a prompt to add the subscription.

3

Click "Add Calendar"

Google Calendar will show a preview of the calendar. Click Add Calendar to subscribe. Your team's games will appear in Google Calendar on all devices where you're signed in.
